Record a short Loom video answering the questions below so our team can evaluate your English fluency and teaching skills. It only takes 3–5 minutes.

🎯 Your Interview Questions

Answer in English. Speak naturally — we're evaluating your fluency, vocabulary, and confidence, not a rehearsed script.

1
Tell me about yourself and your experience teaching English.
We want to know your background, certifications, and how long you've been teaching.
2
Describe a challenging situation you've faced in a classroom and how you handled it.
Show us your problem-solving skills and how you connect with students.
3
How would you explain the difference between "have been doing" and "have done" to a 10-year-old?
We evaluate how you simplify grammar in a way kids can understand.
💡
Tips for a great recording
  • Use a quiet room with good lighting facing your face
  • Aim for 3 to 5 minutes total — don't over-prepare
  • Enable both camera and microphone in Loom
  • Speak naturally, as if talking to a student
📋 Cómo grabar y enviar tu video

Sólo 4 pasos y listo — elige la herramienta que prefieras abajo.

1
Elige una herramienta y graba

Haz clic en una de las opciones de abajo. Vidyard no requiere descargas. YouTube lo puedes grabar directo desde tu teléfono.

2
Responde las 3 preguntas de arriba

Respóndelas en inglés de forma natural. No necesitas ser perfecto — solo ser tú mismo.

3
Copia el link de tu video

Vidyard y YouTube generan un link compartible automáticamente. En YouTube, súbelo como "No listado" para que solo tú y nosotros lo veamos.

4
Pégalo en tu perfil de Maestro

Ve a tu perfil en Moonsforest, abre el campo "Video de Entrevista", pega el link y guarda. ¡Listo!
